Bugatti „Classique“

With more than 7 years of expertise and a profound knowledge especially for the classic models like the EB110 or the Veyron, we are able to offer an all-round Bugatti service and Bugatti repairs in highest quality. More modern models of the French manufacturer are also part of our Bugatti service in Munich.

McLaren „Heritage“

With more than 11 years of McLaren expertise and thus the greatest knowledge of the English brand since the introduction of the first road car, the McLaren MP4-12C, we are able to offer a unique McLaren service and superlative McLaren repairs to the full extent for all models and series. Adaptations and modifications are of course also part of our portfolio.

Ferrari „Tradizione“

With more than 6 years of expertise mainly in the field of classic models from 1987 to 2013, we are able to apply our in-depth knowledge to guarantee the best Ferrari service and Ferrari repairs.
Furthermore, we work hand in hand with one of the most renowned Italian Ferrari dealers in the world in the field of Ferrari restoration and Ferrari certification. Traditionally Italian.

Lamborghini „Classico“

With more than 6 years of expertise and a profound knowledge of the classic models from 1990 to 2013, we are able to offer an all-round Lamborghini service and Lamborghini repairs of the highest quality. Our portfolio naturally also includes special models of the older and younger generations from Italy.
